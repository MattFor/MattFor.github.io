About Relaxy

Relaxy is a multipurpose bot that can keep track of levels, provide modlogs, automatic forums, live reaction moderation panels, polls, and more. Made by a single person (mattfor/MattFor#9884/ me :D) as a passion project at the start of the pandemic in 2019. It is being worked on almost every single day!

Main Features

    Incredibly advanced censoring

    Custom forums with detailed access control

    Multiple different heartboards with customizations

    An advanced moderator log with over 30 events
